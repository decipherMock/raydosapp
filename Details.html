Our General Services

Hospital care.

This service comes in handy to provide medications unavailable at treating hospital for a relative of a customer on admission at a hospital in our catchment area. This service assures timely delivery of such medications to clients’ relatives.

Corporate service.

Raydos Pharmacy has an agreement with a number of corporate bodies in its catchment area to provide pharmaceutical products and services to staff and their dependants. The benefits of this arrangement include affordable prices, flexible payment terms and a dedicated pharmacist to offer pharmaceutical care services free of charge

Refill of prescription reminder service

By this service clients on treatment for chronic condition and registered on our database are sent reminder notices to come and refill their medication or authorise delivery of the refilled medication to them at home

Executive service.

This is a unique delivery service to busy top executives who do not most often have the time to come to our retail outlet to purchase the medicines they need. By this service prescriptions are sent electronically, processed and delivered to clients by courier. This saves time for these individuals and promotes medicine security

Delivery service..

By our courier service we are able to deliver medicinal products to our clients in the comfort of their homes and offices

Raydos Pharmacy is a growing indigenous Ghanaian Pharmaceutical Company located at Amasaman in Accra, Ghana and incorporated in December, 2010. The company was established and managed by Dr. Emmanuel Kuekey, a pharmacist with over twenty years of experience and the wife Susana Kuekey, an experienced educationist also with over twenty years’ experience. Raydos Pharmacy is a wholesale/retail business that is focused on distribution of high quality pharmaceutical and allied healthcare products to Retail Pharmacies, private, public and quasi- government and faith-based health facilities throughout Ghana.
