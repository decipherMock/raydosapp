What does "take with food" or take on an empty stomach" really mean ?

When dosing instructions indicate to "take with food", it is recommended that you take the medication with a meal to avoid stomach or intestinal irritation other times, it is recommended that a medication taken with certain meals or to increase amount of medicine absorptions by your body.When instructed to "take an empty stomach" medication should be taken 3 hours before or 2 hours after eating
